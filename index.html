Application Steps

Easy Step Guide to Study Malaysia

  • Choose Your Course

    The most important consideration when choosing your course is asking yourself why you are looking to study. People should choose preferable university and course to full fill the goal.

  • Submit Your Application

    When you have chosen your preferable subject and university. Apply university to make your dreams true.

  • Accept Offer Letter

    After submitting application please wait for getting offer letter. if the required documents match with university criteria you will get offer letter.

  • Prepare Your Documents

    Once you get the offer letter please prepare your needy documents like (passport,photo,education certificate and transcript and others)

  • Apply For EMGS

    Education Malaysia global services. International students will need a student visa that’s why to get visa approval letter we have to apply at EMGS

  • Apply For Student Visa

    Once you get visa approval letter from EMGS, Then you have to go to Malaysian high commission in your own country to apply student pass
